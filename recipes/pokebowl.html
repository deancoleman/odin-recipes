Poke Bowl

Description

This is one of my favorite dishes to make, its very simple and very filling

Most of the ingredients found in this recipe can be found in your produce aisle

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of brown/white rice
  • 1 carrot
  • 1/2 red onion
  • 1 cucumber
  • 1 cup of red cabbage, shredded
  • 1 block of firm tofu
  • 1 tbsp of cilantro
  • 1/2 an avocado

Instructions

  1. Cook some rice
  2. While your rice is cooking, cut your carrots into long strips
  3. Slice your cucumbers into discs
  4. Dice your onions, shred your cabbage
  5. Once the rice is done, put some into a bowl, then throw on your ingredients
  6. Next we're going to make the tofu, here's the link to my pan fried tofu recipe
  7. Now we're going to whip up a quick sriracha aoili, take some white rice vinegar, sriracha, garlic powder, onion powder, egg free mayo, and about a tbsp of water, combine into a bowl and mix
  8. Everything should be ready now, slice up your avocado and throw it into the bowl along with your tofu. Drizze your sauce on top of the bowl, then chop up some green onions and cilantro for garnish, if you want you can also throw some everything bagel seasoning on top for some extra flavor.

Air Fryer Roasted Potatoes

Description

Very quick and easy dish, its also very filling

One of the cheapest things you can make if you're looking for a healthy dish

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Red Onions
  • Garlic Powder
  • Onion Powder
  • Cumin
  • Red Pepper Flakes
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Italian Seasoning
  • Olive Oil

Instructions

  1. Cut potatoes and red onions into chunks, set the red onions aside
  2. Put the potatoes and every other ingredient in a bowl, mix together nicely
  3. put into air fryer at 400Â° for 15 minutes
  4. Take out of the air fryer, move the potatoes around so that they are heated evenly, then add the red onions
  5. Put everything back into the air fryer for 5 more mins

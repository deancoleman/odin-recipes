Pan Fried Tofu

Description

My go-to tofu recipe, very solid marinade, try this sh*t out when you get a chance

Ingredients are simple too

Ingredients

  • 1 block of tofu
  • maple syrup
  • olive oil
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • soy sauce

Instructions

  1. Combine maple syrup, olive oil, garlic powder, onion powder, and soy sauce into a marinade
  2. Cut your block of tofu into long slabs then place into your marinade
  3. Let it sit for about 15-30 min
  4. Fry on a pan in medium/high heat for about 4 min on each side

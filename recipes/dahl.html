Red Lentil Dahl

Description

Red Lentil Dahl is a quick and easy indian dish that is also low cost and filling.

It features a few simple ingredients that can be found at your local grocery store.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup red lentils
  • 2 tbsp cilantro
  • 4 tomatoes, chopped
  • 3 oniions, chopped
  • 2 serrano peppers, minced
  • 6 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp ground cumin
  • 1 tbsp curry powder
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 can of coconut milk
  • salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Sautee your onions on medium heat until they turn transulecent
  2. Add in your minced garlic and minced serrano peppers
  3. Add in your seasonnings, let it sautee for a bit
  4. Mix in your tomatoes, coconut milk, and red lentils
  5. Cover with the lid, let it sit until it starts to boil, then turn the heat to low and let it sit for 40-60 min
  6. Garnish with cilantro, serve with naan and rice
